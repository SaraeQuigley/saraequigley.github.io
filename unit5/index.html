Hello there!

I don't claim to be a photographer. I'm just regular gal who enjoys seeing the world, and hopes to see every last inch of her at some point.


Having spent a lot of time traveling the world, often alone, I found myself wanting to share all the beauty I experienced with friends back home. Finding words could not do justice, I added a DSLR camera to my packing list, and learned the world and how to use my camera together.


The scenes I've captured are often out of focus or shaky-handed, but once in a while one is just right.
